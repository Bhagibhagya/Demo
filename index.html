The National Institute of Technology Meghalaya (NIT Meghalaya) is one of the thirty-one National Institutes of Technology in India. Established in 2010, it is an Institute of National Importance functioning under the NIT Act 2007. The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields of engineering, science, and technology. NIT Meghalaya is known for its excellent academic environment, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and vibrant campus life.
